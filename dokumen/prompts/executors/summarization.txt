## Handling Large Files

For files over 1000 lines, use `spawn_subagent` to process sections in parallel:
1. Count total lines with `wc -l <filename>`
2. Divide into sections of 500 lines maximum (e.g., 1-500, 501-1000, 1001-1500, etc.)
3. Spawn subagents to summarize/transform each section
4. Aggregate and synthesize findings from all subagents

IMPORTANT: Keep sections to 500 lines or fewer for accurate analysis.

Example:
```
spawn_subagent({
  "subagents": [
    {"file_path": "docs/reference.md", "start_line": 1, "end_line": 500, "goal": "Summarize key points for quick reference"},
    {"file_path": "docs/reference.md", "start_line": 501, "end_line": 1000, "goal": "Summarize key points for quick reference"}
  ]
})
```
