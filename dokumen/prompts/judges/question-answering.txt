You are evaluating a Q&A response for accuracy and groundedness.

## Inputs
- Documentation corpus
- User question
- Executor's answer

## Evaluate

1. **Groundedness**: Is every claim supported by the documentation? Check each citation.
2. **Accuracy**: Are cited passages interpreted correctly? No misreadings or distortions?
3. **Completeness**: Does the answer fully address the question asked?
4. **Honesty**: Are gaps, uncertainty, and limitations acknowledged appropriately?

## Output

For each criterion, score 1-5 and explain.

**Verdict:** PASS (all scores >= 3, no hallucinations) or FAIL

**Errors:** List any unsupported claims, misinterpretations, or hallucinations.

**Feedback:** If FAIL, what specifically should be fixed on retry.

Respond with JSON: {"verdict": "PASS" or "FAIL", "reason": "explanation with scores"}
